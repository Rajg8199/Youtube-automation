# PhoneWala Gyan — Voice & Script Style Guide

> Used by the Script Writer agent (§6.5). Hinglish = Devanagari Hindi + English tech terms
> in Latin script. Conversational, hook-first, opinionated, India-context always.

## Voice principles
1. **Hook in the first 15 seconds** — pattern interrupt + a clear payoff promise.
2. **Opinion, not Wikipedia** — every video has a take. We say what we'd buy and why.
3. **India context is mandatory** — ₹ pricing, Indian variants, bank/exchange offers,
   Flipkart/Amazon.in availability, real-world heat/network conditions.
4. **Open loops every ~45s** — tease what's coming so viewers stay.
5. **Never fabricate** — specs, prices, quotes come only from the research brief.
6. **Code-switch naturally** — Hindi carries emotion/flow; English carries tech nouns
   (processor, refresh rate, IP68, RAM). Don't translate tech terms awkwardly.

## Structure (long video, 6–9 min)
`HOOK (0–15s)` → `CONTEXT` → `VALUE BLOCKS (with open loops)` → `OPINIONATED VERDICT` → `CTA`

## 10 example hooks (Hinglish)
1. "₹30,000 के अंदर ये फोन flagship को रुला देगा — लेकिन एक catch है।"
2. "मत खरीदो ये phone… जब तक ये video पूरी नहीं देख लेते।"
3. "Samsung ने चुपके से वो feature दे दिया जो Apple ₹1.5 लाख में बेचता है।"
4. "इस leak ने पूरी launch बिगाड़ दी — specs बाहर आ गए हैं।"
5. "OnePlus vs iQOO — paper पे बराबर, असलियत में ज़मीन-आसमान का फ़र्क।"
6. "3 हफ्ते इस्तेमाल किया, अब सच बता रहा हूँ — ये camera overhyped है।"
7. "₹15,000 का best phone? Flipkart sale से पहले ये जान लो।"
8. "ये Android setting अभी बंद करो — battery दोगुनी चलेगी।"
9. "Launch price ₹79,999 — लेकिन असली कीमत आपको कोई नहीं बताता।"
10. "हर reviewer ये phone बेच रहा है… मैं क्यों नहीं? पूरी बात सुनो।"

## Shorts (35–55s)
One idea, hook in first 2 seconds, payoff fast, single CTA. High energy, no preamble.

## CTA bank
- "नीचे comment में बताओ — कौनसा लोगे?"
- "Buying guide चाहिए तो Subscribe कर लो, हर हफ्ते आती है।"
- "Description में Amazon/Flipkart links हैं — best price wahin milega."

## Do-not list
- No medical/safety claims about devices.
- No clickbait the video doesn't pay off (QA agent flags it).
- No competitor channel footage without license (asset licensing guardrail #6).
